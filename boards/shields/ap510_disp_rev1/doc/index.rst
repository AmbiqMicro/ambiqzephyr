Apollo510 Display Card Rev1
###############

Overview
********

Support for Apollo510 expansion boards (shields)
controller.

Apollo510 Host Shield
*****************************

Pins Assignment of the Shield Connector
=======================================

+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| Shield Connector Pin  | Function           |   Apollo510 Pin  |
+=======================+====================+==================+
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-1                  | DISP_CSX           | 209              |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-2                  | DISP_TE            | 33               |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-3                  | DISP_QSPI_SCLK     | 81               |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-4                  | DISP_RSTn          | 63               |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-5                  | DISP_QSPI_D0       | 79               |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-6                  | TOUCH_SDA          | 26               |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-7                  | DISP_QSPI_D1       | 80               |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-8                  | TOUCH_SCL          | 25               |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-9                  | DISP_QSPI_D2       | 82               |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-10                 | TOUCH_INT          | 77               |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-11                 | DISP_QSPI_D3       | 83               |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+
| J1-12                 | TOUCH_RST          | 78               |
+-----------------------+--------------------+------------------+

Programming
***********

Set ``--shield ap510_disp_rev1`` when you invoke ``west build`` or ``cmake``
in your Zephyr application.
